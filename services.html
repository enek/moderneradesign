Book an Hour Design/Color Consultation.
“The Beginning”

Meeting with you in person is an opportunity to convert what you would like to achieve into specific projects that fit within your budget as well prepare the foundation for transforming your ideas into a complete interior. In some cases our interior design consultation may be all you need to get yourself started, if you are planning to do the work yourself. During the 1 hour consultation, we would review your space, discuss expectations, functions, styles, colors, trends, lighting. We will try to answer any other design dilemmas that you may have. $200 for an hour consultation. If you decide that you’ll need further assistance, we charge an hourly fee of $150. Depending on the scope of work and want an estimate of how much a project would cost, a package can be made up for you.
